Prompt Builder – Question Guide

This page explains each question in detail so users understand how to answer effectively and generate high-quality AI prompts.

Q1. What task do you want the AI to perform?

Explanation:
This defines the main goal of the prompt. The AI needs a clear and direct task so it knows exactly what you want.

Example Answer:
Write a 200-word summary of a research paper.

Q2. What background/context should the AI know before generating the output?

Explanation:
Context helps the AI understand the situation, purpose, audience, and style. More context usually results in better accuracy.

Example Answer:
The summary is for college students who are new to machine learning.

Q3. What references, examples, or constraints should the AI consider?

Explanation:
References guide tone and structure. Constraints define limits such as length, format, or style.

Example Answer:
Use simple language. Mention only three key insights. Avoid equations.

Q4. How should the AI evaluate whether the output is good or not?

Explanation:
This tells the AI what quality means for your task. It helps the AI self-check before finalizing the response.

Example Answer:
The output is good if it is clear, accurate, and easy to read.

Q5. What improvements should the AI make if the first output is not perfect?

Explanation:
This guides the AI on how to refine its response. It ensures the second attempt is better than the first.

Example Answer:
Improve the flow, shorten long sentences, and add bullet points if needed.
